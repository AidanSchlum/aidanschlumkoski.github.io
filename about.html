My name is Aidan Schlumkoski I am currently a second-year graphic design student at Conestoga College in the Graphic Design Advanced Diploma Program. I am a cheerful spirit with a genuine love for life and design. I am highly driven, passionate designer with a love for branding, specifically for sports teams and athletic events, especially motion graphics. I have worked with clients including; the City of Cambridge and the Early Childhood Professional Resource Centre on branding projects. In 2022, I received recognition for my motion design work on a FreshCo Christmas campaign. I am is proficient with various Adobe software including Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, and AfterEffects.
