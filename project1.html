About the Project

The Buffalo Sabres are positioning themselves to become a perennial playoff contender in the NHL and their performance in recent seasons has shown a steady improvement in various categories. The goal of this infographic was to take the statistics and organize them in a visually intriguing way that would appeal to the target demographic of sports fans. The poster was designed in Illustrator as opposed to InDesign as I wanted to challenge myself to not rely on a grid and focus on creating the infographic using the principles of design (contrast, balance, emphasis, proportion, hierarchy, and unity).
